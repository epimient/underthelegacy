Ashes of Eternity

In production

Ashes of Eternity is the debut EP by Under the Legacy. A five-track ritual carved in distortion, repetition and decay.

The record explores themes of mortality, inner collapse and the persistence of memory, unfolding slowly like smoke trapped in a forgotten room.

Tracklist (Provisional)

  1. Invocation
  2. Static Prayer
  3. Perdition
  4. Haunted by Shadows
  5. The Eternal

First Single

Haunted by Shadows will be released as the first glimpse into the world of Ashes of Eternity.

A slow-burning track built around hypnotic riffs and lyrics that deal with obsession, presence and the weight of unseen forces.
